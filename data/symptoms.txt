acne:
  what = Blocked oil glands and dead skin cells causing pimples or cysts.
  why = Hormonal changes increase oil production and clog pores.
  red_flags = Painful deep cysts, scarring, no improvement after weeks.

irregular_periods:
  what = Menstrual cycles that are unpredictable in timing or flow.
  why = Hormonal imbalance, stress, weight change or PCOS.
  red_flags = No periods for 3 months, very heavy bleeding, severe pain.

period_cramps:
  what = Lower abdominal pain during menstruation.
  why = Strong uterine contractions caused by prostaglandins.
  red_flags = Pain stopping daily activity, worsening over time.

mood_swings:
  what = Rapid emotional changes like irritability or sadness.
  why = Hormonal shifts, stress and poor sleep.
  red_flags = Persistent low mood, thoughts of self harm.

stress:
  what = Mental and physical strain from pressure or overload.
  why = Continuous activation of stress hormones like cortisol.
  red_flags = Constant hopelessness, panic, inability to function.

sleep_problems:
  what = Difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep or feeling rested.
  why = Screen exposure, stress, caffeine and irregular routine.
  red_flags = Insomnia lasting weeks, daytime impairment.

irritability:
  what = Being easily annoyed or angered.
  why = Stress, hunger, lack of sleep, emotional overload.
  red_flags = Aggression, harming others, loss of control.

anxiety:
  what = Excessive worry or fear interfering with daily life.
  why = Overactive stress and fear circuits in the brain.
  red_flags = Panic attacks, avoidance, self harm thoughts.

appetite_change:
  what = Eating much less or more than usual.
  why = Stress hormones and emotional states affecting hunger.
  red_flags = Rapid weight change, bingeing or restricting.

weight_change:
  what = Noticeable gain or loss of body weight.
  why = Metabolic, hormonal, lifestyle or emotional factors.
  red_flags = Sudden unexplained change, weakness, palpitations.

fatigue:
  what = Persistent tiredness not relieved by rest.
  why = Poor sleep, stress, anemia or illness.
  red_flags = Breathlessness, pallor, long lasting exhaustion.

facial_hair:
  what = Unusual increase or decrease in facial hair growth.
  why = Hormonal imbalance especially androgen levels.
  red_flags = Sudden coarse hair with irregular periods.

nightfall:
  what = Involuntary ejaculation during sleep in males.
  why = Normal hormonal activity during puberty.
  red_flags = Pain, distress or infection signs.

genital_discomfort:
  what = Itching, burning or irritation in genital area.
  why = Infection, irritation, allergy or hygiene issues.
  red_flags = Pain, discharge, odor, sores or fever.
